Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-16

1. Who We Are

SETKI Grid ("SETKI", "we", "our") is a data grid widget for Zoho CRM, developed and operated by an independent developer. You can contact us at: dev@setki.dev

2. What Data We Process

2.1 Zoho CRM Data

SETKI accesses records from your Zoho CRM account (contacts, leads, deals, and other modules you configure) via the official Zoho CRM API in order to display them in the grid widget. SETKI does not persistently store your CRM records. Data is accessed directly from Zoho and rendered in your browser. SETKI does not retain CRM records beyond what is necessary to display them during your active session.

2.2 Seat Assignment Data

When an administrator assigns Pro seats to Zoho users, seat assignment data is stored within Zoho's infrastructure and is subject to Zoho's own privacy policy. This data is managed by Zoho and can be removed by your administrator at any time.

2.3 Purchase and Billing Data

Pro licenses are sold through LemonSqueezy (Lemon Squeezy LLC), a third-party payment processor. Billing information (such as name, email, and payment details) is collected and managed by LemonSqueezy. SETKI receives only a license key and subscription status.

LemonSqueezy's privacy policy: lemonsqueezy.com/privacy

2.4 Technical and Operational Logs

We maintain technical logs for security monitoring, error diagnosis, and license validation. These logs may include IP addresses, timestamps, and license key identifiers. Logs are processed on servers located in the United States. Data collected in logs is not used for marketing or profiling purposes.

3. How We Use Data

We use the data described above solely to:

  • —Provide and operate the SETKI widget functionality
  • —Validate Pro licenses and manage seat assignments
  • —Maintain the security and integrity of our service

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

5. International Data Transfers

Technical operational data may be processed on servers located in the United States. By using SETKI, you acknowledge that your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your own.

6. Data Retention

  • —CRM data — not retained beyond the active session.
  • —Seat assignments — managed within Zoho's infrastructure; can be removed by your administrator at any time.
  • —Billing data — retained by LemonSqueezy per their policy.
  • —Technical logs — retained for a limited period for operational and security purposes.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data. To submit any privacy-related request or question, please contact us at dev@setki.dev and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Children's Privacy

SETKI is a B2B product intended for use by businesses and their employees. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under the age of 16.
